  • sensitivity

    The sensitivity metric determines how far a gesture must travel, in unit coordinates, from the initial tap point before control tracking begins.

    The lower this value, the quicker the control surface will begin tracking a pan gesture.

    Note

    Sensitivty is given in the unit points, therefore, a value greater than one is capped.

    Warning

    Setting this value below zero is undefined behavior.

  • Direction

    Directions that define movement axes of the control surface. Raw values are rotation angles expressed in degrees.

    • North: Towards the top of the screen.
    • East: Towards the right of the screen.
    • South: Towards the botton of the screen.
    • West: Towards the left of the screen.
